Wallzy Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters.

Wallzy is designed to provide a secure, premium, and modern wallpaper experience while respecting your privacy.

Information We Collect

Wallzy does not collect personally identifiable information such as your name, phone number, or payment information.

Wallpapers, thumbnails, and media assets are delivered from remote servers. Internet access is required for browsing and downloading wallpapers.

Wallzy may locally store cached thumbnails, favorites, and app settings on your device to improve performance and user experience.

Third-Party Services

These third-party services may collect limited technical information in accordance with their own privacy policies.

Data Security

We aim to use reasonable technical and organizational measures to help protect the app experience and locally stored information.
